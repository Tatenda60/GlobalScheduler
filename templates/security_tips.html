Security Tips

Security Best Practices

Protecting your account and financial information is our top priority. Follow these tips to keep your information secure:

Password Security
Strong Password

Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.

Regular Updates

Change your password periodically for better security.

Unique Passwords

Use different passwords for different accounts.

Password Requirements:
  • At least 8 characters long
  • Includes at least one uppercase letter
  • Includes at least one lowercase letter
  • Includes at least one number
  • Includes at least one special character (e.g., !@#$%^&*)
Password Tip

Consider using a passphrase - a sequence of words that is easy for you to remember but difficult for others to guess.

Account Verification
Verify Email

Keep your email up to date to receive important notifications.

Phone Verification

Add your phone number for additional account recovery options.

Why Verification Matters:
  • Helps confirm your identity
  • Enables account recovery if you forget your password
  • Allows us to notify you of suspicious activities
  • Ensures you receive important updates about your loan applications
Important

We will never ask for your password or personal information through email. If you receive such messages, please report them immediately.

Login Security
Device Management

Always log out when using public computers.

Monitor Activity

Regularly check your account activity and report suspicious behavior.

Additional Tips:
  • Use secure networks when accessing your account
  • Keep your operating system and browser updated
  • Install and maintain antivirus software on your devices
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts that mimic our website
Did You Know?

Most security breaches occur due to weak passwords or social engineering rather than sophisticated hacking techniques.
